Winterlude: A Winter Wonderland Like No Other

Glide along the world-renowned Rideau Canal Skateway, witness the artistry of ice sculptures taking shape, and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Winterlude Village, where warmth, laughter, and culinary delights await.

Unleash the Spirit of Winter

Experience the World's Longest Skateway: Glide along the iconic Rideau Canal Skateway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and soak in the breathtaking scenery of Ottawa's picturesque waterfront.

Adventure Awaits

Embrace the Thrill of Ice Sports: Challenge yourself to a game of ice hockey or curling at one of the festival's ice rinks. For those seeking a more adrenaline-pumping experience, try your hand at snowshoeing or cross-country skiing through the picturesque NCC Greenbelt.
