Rue Sparks
Rue Sparks St., Ottawa, ON K1A 0X8
Sparks St is a great take at night. The pedestrian only street is lined with ice sculptures that are lit up at night for a beautiful colorful effect. Several things for the kids such as skating on the canal (ice conditions permitting), tubing and zip lines.

350 Rue Laurier, Gatineau, QC K1A 0M5
Discover winter with your family in the huge playground. Slide down the snow slides, see beautiful snow sculptures during the day and experience the magic of the site at night as the sculptures are illuminated. Take part in a variety of outdoor activities.

February 3-20, 2024
Rideau Canal Skateway
1 Second Ave, Ottawa, ON K1S 2H2
Each winter, the NCC transforms the historic Rideau Canal into the world’s largest skating rink. The Rideau Canal Skateway winds its way through the heart of the National Capital over a total length of 7.8 km. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Rideau Canal in winter becomes a treasure that is the pride of the National Capital and a signature destination.
