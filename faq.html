Frequently Asked Questions

Winterlude Essentials: Get Answers to Your Winter Festival Questions

What is Winterlude?
Winterlude is an annual winter festival held in Ottawa, Ontario, and Gatineau, Quebec. It is one of Canada's largest winter festivals, attracting over 750,000 visitors each year. The festival features a wide variety of activities, including ice skating, snowshoeing, skiing, snowboarding, and ice carving. It typically runs for three weeks in February. The exact dates for the 2024 festival have not yet been announced.
How can I get to Winterlude?
Ottawa and Gatineau are easily accessible by car, train, or plane. The nearest airports are Ottawa Macdonald-Cartier International Airport (YOW) and Gatineau-Ottawa Executive Airport (CYND).
Where can I park during Winterlude?
There are many parking options available throughout Ottawa and Gatineau. Some of the most popular parking lots include the Rideau Centre parking garage, the Westin Ottawa parking garage, and the Lansdowne Park parking lot.
Where can I stay during Winterlude?
There are many hotels, bed and breakfasts, and vacation rentals available in Ottawa and Gatineau. Some of the most popular hotels include the Fairmont Chateau Laurier, the Westin Ottawa, and the Delta Hotels by Marriott Ottawa City Centre.
What are some of the activities at Winterlude?
Winterlude features a wide variety of activities, including:
  • Ice skating on the Rideau Canal Skateway, the world's longest skating rink
  • Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing in the NCC Greenbelt
  • Skiing and snowboarding at Mont Cascades and Camp Fortune
  • Ice carving demonstrations and competitions
  • Ice slides, snow forts, and other winter-themed activities for kids
  • Live music and entertainment at various locations throughout the city
    • Are there any free activities at Winterlude?
    Yes, there are many free activities at Winterlude.
    How much does it cost to attend Winterlude?
    Many of the Winterlude events are free to attend, including the Rideau Canal Skateway, the ice sculptures, and the Crystal Garden. However, there are some paid events, such as the Winterlude Village, the Festival of Winter Cultures, and the Winterlude Lights Tour. Prices for paid events vary depending on the event.
    What should I wear to Winterlude?
    It is important to dress warmly for Winterlude, as the temperatures can be cold. You should wear layers of clothing, including a warm jacket, hat, gloves, and scarf. You may also want to wear snow pants or waterproof boots.
    What should I eat at Winterlude?
    There are many food vendors and restaurants located at Winterlude, offering a variety of cuisines. You can find everything from traditional Canadian fare to international cuisine.
    Is Winterlude accessible?
    A: Winterlude is committed to making the festival accessible to everyone. There are many accessible activities and facilities available, including the Rideau Canal Skateway, which is snow-covered and can be accessed by wheelchairs. There are also accessible washrooms and parking spaces available.

    Escape the ordinary and embrace the magic of winter at Winterlude, where the spirit of adventure and the warmth of community come together to create a winter wonderland like no other.
